Andrew Chang

Cell, Molecular, Developmental Biology

I am currently a 4th year student attending University of California, Riverside majoring in Cell, Molecular, and Developmental Biology. I plan to graduate in Spring 2023 and intend to attend professional school and work in the healthcare field after I graduate. More specifically, I hope to attend dental school and eventually establish my own practice.

I have spent over 300 hours volunteering at Riverside Community Hospital, working with nurses and other hospital staff to help treat patients and improve their overall stay while they recover. I have volunteered in several departments including Radiology, Med-Surg, and an outpatient center for cancer patients. I intend to search for shadowing positions under both medical professionals and dentists to gain further experience prior to attending professional school.

In my free time I enjoy hanging out with friends, playing the guitar and piano, and spending time in the gym. Additionally, I help a couple of friends manage their start-up business. I have also been active in a few clubs on campus, even taking leadership positions in the Korean American Student Association where I was the Activities chair for one year and Secretary the year after. I plan on continuing refining my skills and helping my friends even after achieving my goals.
